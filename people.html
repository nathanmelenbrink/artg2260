Final Projects

GeoTales: The Quest is Real: by Sara Hartleben, Elijah Cobb and Isa Diaz
An application that uses Google Maps + Street View for interactive urban storytelling! Follow along with your phone to preset stories all around Boston (or create your own adventure!)

What in the Night Sky? by Nicolette Tovstashy and Kirill Zavyalov
Test the accuracy with which you can draw the 88 constellations of the night sky. This app uses an evolutionary algorithm to detect which constellation you've drawn, and then gives you a score for accuracy.

Arctic Adventures: by Anna Hegyaljai, Manvi Tandon and Louis Dechamps
Learn about the ways in which climate change affects the wildlife of the arctic circle through a collection of fun educational games.

Imagine: by Karisa Tzeng and Alexander Nathanson
An interactive music video set to the 1971 classic by John Lennon.

Snooze Helper: by Erin Wang, Shannon Hanlon, Jacob Krawczyk, and Jaida Mercurio
Visualize your sleep quality over time (enter data manually or sync with the SleepCycle App). Track additional fields (e.g. caffeinated beverages or exercise) to see how they affect your sleep trends.
